Impact of Latitude on Weather

How does latitude impact observed temperature? In a previous project we looked at a random data set of over 500 cities across the globe to view for ourselves the results. This data came from OpenWeatherMap and we accessed it through their API.

Once we had downloaded the data (making sure to not hit the API too frequently, we sliced and diced the data and plotted it utilizing Matplotlib. Along with temperature, we looked at humidity, cloudiness and wind speed.
